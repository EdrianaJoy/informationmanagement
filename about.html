Overview

When seeking employment opportunities, certain employers require applicants to fill out an application when applying for jobs. A General Employment Application, or a Job Application form, is a standardized business document designed to efficiently collect necessary and relevant information from job applicants. Its purpose is to assist recruiters and employers in determining and selecting suitable candidates for specific roles within the company. This form typically includes required fields, such as personal information, educational background, and employment history. Additionally, it includes a section for applicants' job preferences or specific employment requirements, further streamlining the hiring process.

General employment application forms are widely used across industries and businesses of all sizes, showcasing their versatility in the hiring process. Whether it's a small restaurant hiring a server or a multinational corporation filling multiple positions, these forms are essential for candidate evaluation. In retail, they help assess customer service skills and availability, while in professional services, they identify candidates with necessary qualifications. In healthcare and manufacturing, they ensure compliance with industry regulations. Regardless of the sector, these forms streamline hiring, helping employers find the right fit for their needs.

Business Rules

1. This application portal is for the sole purpose of finding interesting applicants. If the employer is interested, they will still contact the applicant for their resume or other necessary requirements.

2. A person must submit at least one application request to be considered an applicant and may submit multiple times, but their prior application should have been approved or rejected before being able to reapply again.

3. An applicant can only apply for one position per application in KERA company.

4. The KERA company only accepts one valid address, phone number, and email address.

5. The KERA company wants to know the applicant's work history for Background Overview and Personal Quality Analysis.

6. An applicant must have at least two years of work experience and can place multiple previous job experiences they had so far.

7. Applicants must at least have a bachelor's degree or higher and can input multiple degrees they have acquired so far from different or the same institutions.

8. Only people who graduated from institutions within the National Capital Region can apply.

9. The KERA company prefers applicants from institutions such as the University of the Philippines, University of Santo Tomas, Ateneo de Manila University, De La Salle University, and the Polytechnic University of the Philippines.

10. An applicant must at least have one skill relevant to the position they are applying for.

11. The KERA company prefers applicants whose previous work experience specializes in {insert skill name}.
